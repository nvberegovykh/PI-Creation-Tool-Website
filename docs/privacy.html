1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email, username, profile metadata, avatar, and account status.
  • Content data: messages, media uploads, comments, reactions, shared assets, and project submissions.
  • Interaction data: play events, engagement signals, clicks, likes, and recommendation-relevant activity.
  • Technical data: device/browser metadata, app version, log events, network diagnostics, and security-related telemetry.
  • Support/commercial data: inquiries, request forms, and project communication records.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide, operate, and improve platform features, including messaging, media playback, and collaboration.
  • Deliver personalized feeds/recommendations and improve relevance/performance.
  • Protect users and services through moderation, abuse detection, and fraud/security controls.
  • Communicate account, transactional, and support updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

3. Legal Bases

Depending on jurisdiction, we process personal data under one or more of: contract necessity, legitimate interests, consent, and legal obligation.

4. How We Share Data

  • Service providers and infrastructure vendors (hosting, storage, communications, analytics, security).
  • Other users, where sharing is inherent to selected features (e.g., messaging, public posts, shared content).
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law or valid legal process.
  • Business transfer participants in case of merger, acquisition, financing, or asset sale, subject to safeguards.

We do not sell personal data in the traditional broker sense.
